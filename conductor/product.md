# Initial Concept
CouponCupid is an app designed to help users compare prices between grocery stores, view available coupons, and compile grocery lists.

# Target Audience
CouponCupid caters to a diverse range of users, primarily focusing on:

*   **Savvy Shoppers:** Individuals who are dedicated to finding the best deals, comparing prices across various stores, and maximizing their savings on groceries. They are often proactive in their shopping approach.
*   **Busy Parents:** Users who require efficient tools to manage their household grocery shopping. They benefit from quick list creation and the ability to easily identify savings that fit their demanding schedules.
*   **Budget-Conscious Individuals:** Shoppers whose primary goal is to minimize their grocery expenses. They actively use the app to track spending, leverage coupons, and make informed purchasing decisions to stay within their budget.
